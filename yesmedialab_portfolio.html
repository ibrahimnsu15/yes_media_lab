Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is YouTube automation?
+
YouTube automation is the process of running a profitable YouTube channel using a team-based or AI-driven workflow — without appearing on camera. We handle everything from niche selection and scriptwriting to voiceovers, editing, thumbnail design, and publishing, so you earn passive income from the channel without doing the day-to-day work.
How does AI content creation work?
+
We use advanced AI tools — including image generators, AI voice synthesis, AI writing assistants, and video production platforms — combined with human oversight and editing. The result is high-quality content produced at scale, faster and at a lower cost than traditional production methods, while maintaining your brand's voice and quality standards.
Do you manage social media accounts?
+
Yes! Our Channel & Page Management service covers daily content publishing, community management, audience engagement, analytics reporting, growth strategy, and brand consistency — across YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, and other platforms. We become an extension of your team.
Can you provide script writing services only?
+
Absolutely. We offer standalone script writing services for YouTube documentaries, Shorts, educational content, commercial scripts, and personal brand storytelling. You can engage us just for scripting and handle production yourself, or use our full end-to-end package.
Do you offer custom packages?
+
Yes — every client is different and we pride ourselves on building custom solutions. During your free consultation, we'll discuss your specific goals, budget, and timeline, then propose a package that's built specifically for your needs rather than a one-size-fits-all offering.
What industries do you work with?
+
We work across a wide range of industries including e-commerce, finance, technology, health & wellness, education, real estate, entertainment, coaching, SaaS, personal branding, and more. Our AI-powered workflows adapt to any niche, and we've successfully managed channels in over 20 different industries.
